我们定义势能为一个位置的函数 $V: \mathbb{R}^n\to\mathbb{R}$ , 满足 $\vec{F}=-\nabla V$ . 容易发现这样的函数在差一个常数的意义下唯一确定. 

**Prop**. 机械能守恒  
*pf.*  由链式法则, $$\frac{dE}{dt}=\frac{dV}{dt}+\frac{dK}{dt}=-\vec{F}\cdot\vec{v}+m\vec{v}\cdot\vec{a}=0$$

**def**. 定义系统的拉格朗日量$$\mathscr{L}=K-V$$我们假设$\mathscr{L}$ 与位置的二阶及以上导数无关(我暂时还不清楚为什么要这么假设)  
对于任意道路 $\vec{q}(t): [t_0,t_1]\to\mathbb{R}^n$, 定义其作用量泛函 $S[q(t)]=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}\mathscr{L}(t,q(t),q'(t))dt$  

为了便于处理, 我们暂时假设运动是一维的.   
**Thm**. Assume $x:[t_0, t_1]\to \mathbb{R}$ is a smooth path that extremizes the action functional
$S=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}\mathscr{L}dt$ for all possible smooth paths $q:[t_0, t_1]\to\mathbb{R}$ with the same end points: $q(t_0) =x(t_0),q(t_1)=x(t_1)$ . Assume the Lagrangian $\mathscr{L}=\mathscr{L}(q(t),q'(t),t)$ only depends on $q(t)$, its time derivative $q'(t)$ and time $t$. Then $x(t)$ satisfies the Euler-Lagrange Equation   $$\frac{d}{dt}\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'}=\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx}$$
*pf.*  设$x(t)$为使 $S$ 最小的道路, 则对于任意 $\gamma:[t_0,t_1]\to\mathbb{R},\ \gamma(t_0)=\gamma(t_1)=0$, $\epsilon$ 为一个小量, 有 $S[x(t)+\epsilon\gamma(t)]>S[x(t)]$. 设 $f(s)=S[x(t)+s\gamma(t)]$, 则$\frac{d}{ds}f(0)=0$.  
$$\begin{aligned}\frac{df}{ds}(0)&=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx}\gamma+\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'}\gamma')dt\\
&=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx}\gamma+\frac{d}{dt}(\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'}\gamma)-(\frac{d}{dt}\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'})\gamma)dt\\
&=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx}\gamma-(\frac{d}{dt}\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'})\gamma)dt+\frac{d}{dt}(\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'}\gamma)\big|_{t_0}^{t_1}\\
&=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx}\gamma-(\frac{d}{dt}\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'})\gamma)dt\\
&=0
\end{aligned}$$
由于 $\gamma$ 的任意性, $\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx}-\frac{d}{dt}\frac{d\mathscr{L}}{dx'}=0$.
